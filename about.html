About the Center

The Center for Analytics and Innovation with Data (CAID) advances evidence‑based policymaking and public‑interest technology through open data, rigorous methods, and collaborative research.

Mission

The Center for Analytics and Innovation with Data (CAID) focuses on applied, data-driven research for the public good. We bring together faculty, students, and industry partners across the University of Denver to translate insights from data and AI into solutions that strengthen democratic governance, innovation, and social impact.

  • Innovation for Research & Learning: Advancing cutting-edge research in data analytics and AI, expanding knowledge while integrating students into transformative educational experiences.
  • Collaborative Impact & Industry Engagement: Fostering interdisciplinary partnerships across academia and industry, connecting students with the Denver community for hands-on opportunities to apply data and AI-driven solutions to real-world challenges.
