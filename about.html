About the Center

The Center for Analytics and Innovation with Data (CAID) advances applied research and experiential learning at the intersection of data, technology, and decision-making. We connect students, faculty, and industry partners to turn data into actionable insights that create value across sectors - from business and education to public and social impact.

Mission

  • Innovation for Research & Learning: Advancing cutting-edge research in data analytics and AI, expanding knowledge while integrating students into transformative educational experiences.
  • Collaborative Impact & Industry Engagement: By fostering partnerships with industry and the Denver community, CAID helps bridge the gap between research and application. We empower students to work alongside faculty and external partners, producing data-driven insights that enhance business performance, drive innovation, and strengthen the analytics ecosystem across sectors.
