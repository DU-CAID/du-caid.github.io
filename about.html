About the Center

The Center for Analytics and Innovation with Data (CAID) advances evidence‑based policymaking and public‑interest technology through open data, rigorous methods, and collaborative research.

What we do

  • Publish open datasets and dashboards for civic transparency
  • Develop reproducible research pipelines
  • Create applied teaching materials and workshops
  • Partner with public agencies and non‑profits
